## About our domains

* **`onetimesecret.com`**: Production portal into regional sites
* **Regional domains** (`eu.onetimesecret.com`, `ca.onetimesecret.com`, etc): Share-nothing application instances with full data sovereignty
* **`onetimesecret.dev`**: Exact mirror of production structure for dev/staging (same BunnyCDN, same DNS patterns)
* **`onetime.co`**: CNAME target for customer custom domains. A direct route to our infrastructure.


## Why no subdomains?

 GitHub Pages learned this the hard way, migrating from `*.github.com` to `github.io` in 2013 after discovering that user-hosted JavaScript could toss cookies to the parent domain.
